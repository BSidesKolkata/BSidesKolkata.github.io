About Bsides Kolkata

Welcome to BSides Kolkata 2023, the inaugural event in the vibrant city of Kolkata, India. As part of the global network of Security BSides conferences, our mission is to foster an inclusive and collaborative platform for information security and privacy education.

Security BSides is renowned for its grassroots approach, aiming to make cybersecurity accessible and community-driven. Since its inception with the first event in Las Vegas in 2009, the movement has grown into a worldwide network of conferences, each dedicated to advancing the industry through shared knowledge and meaningful discussions.

Join us at BSides Kolkata 2023 as we embark on this exciting journey of exploration, collaboration, and empowerment. Together, we’ll shape the future of cybersecurity education in Kolkata and beyond.
